Parties and Premises: The appellant and his father were tenants of adjacent premises in Raikhad Ward, Ahmedabad.
Nature of Business: The premises were leased for conducting a weaving business under the name Ahmedabad Fine Weaving Works.
Partnership Formation: Bharat Neon Signs, a partnership, was formed in October 1960 involving six partners initially, later including the appellant and his father.
Changes in Partnership: Several partnership deeds were executed over the years, detailing changes in partners and profit-sharing arrangements.
Allegations: The landlord alleged non-use of the premises for the intended purpose and unlawful subletting to Bharat Neon Signs.
