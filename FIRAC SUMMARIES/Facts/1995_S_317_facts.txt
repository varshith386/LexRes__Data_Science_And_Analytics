Twelve accused persons were tried for various offences under IPC sections 302, 307, 149, 324, and Section 3 of the Terrorist and Disruptive Activities (Prevention) Act, 1987 (TADA). Six accused were acquitted, while five appellants (Bonkya alias Bharat Shivaji Mane, Mandu Baliba Dombe, Ashok Baloba Dombe, Ranjar Bhausaheb Dombe, and Kaka alias Pandurang Baloba Dombe) were convicted and sentenced for their involvement in an incident resulting in the death of Popat and injuries to PW9 and PW14.
