Three appeals were filed by the State of West Bengal (SLP C No. 401 of 1993), State Fishery Development Corporation Ltd. (SLP C No. 13516 of 1992), and Bansilal Farms (SLP C No. 13314 of 1992) against a judgment of the Division Bench of the Calcutta High Court dated 25th September 1992. The dispute revolves around Nalban Fishery, subject to a partition suit (Suit No. 2539 of 1955) in the Calcutta High Court, resulting in a compromise decree involving members of the Sarkar family.
