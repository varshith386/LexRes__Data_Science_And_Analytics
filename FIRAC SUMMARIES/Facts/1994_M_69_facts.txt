The case involves a civil appeal by special leave against the judgment of the Gujarat High Court.
The plaintiff filed suit for redemption of mortgaged properties.
Defendants claimed they were tenants under the Bombay Tenancy and Agricultural Lands Act, 1948 (BTAL Act).
Trial court and District Court ruled in favor of the plaintiff.
High Court allowed the defendants' appeal, remanding the case for determination of tenancy status by the Mamlatdar.
