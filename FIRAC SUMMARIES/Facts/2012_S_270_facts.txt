Pathan Hussain Basha was married to Pathan Haseena Begum in an arranged marriage on June 23, 2002, in Guntur, with a promised dowry of Rs. 25,000.
Rs. 15,000 was paid at the time of marriage, with a promise to pay Rs. 10,000 by October 2002, which was not fulfilled due to financial constraints.
Allegedly pressured by Pathan Hussain Basha, his father, and mother, Pathan Haseena Begum was harassed for the unpaid dowry.
On February 15, 2003, Pathan Haseena Begum was found dead by hanging in the accused's house. She was declared brought dead at the hospital.
