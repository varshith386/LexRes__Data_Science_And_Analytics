FIR Registration: An FIR was registered on March 23, 1996, based on the statement of Prakashi Devi.
Incident Description: Prakashi Devi stated that on the night of March 22-23, 1996, while she and her daughter-in-law, Sheela Devi, were sleeping, the appellant jumped over the front wall of her house, broke the bulbs, and ran away around 11:30 PM. The appellant returned around 12:30 AM, touched Sheela Devi, who woke up and raised an alarm, causing the appellant to flee.
Police Investigation: The police investigated the complaint and submitted a report under Section 173 of the Code of Criminal Procedure.
Charges Framed: The Judicial Magistrate framed the following charges against the appellant:
House trespass with preparation for causing hurt or assault, punishable under Section 452 IPC.
Assault and use of criminal force against Prakashi Devi with intent to outrage her modesty, punishable under Section 354 IPC.
Appellant's Plea: The appellant pleaded not guilty and claimed trial.
