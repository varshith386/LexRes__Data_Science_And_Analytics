The case involved an appeal concerning the retrospective effect of a registration certificate issued under the Trade Marks Act, 1999. The appellant, M/s. Meghraj Biscuits Industries Ltd., had applied for registration of the trademark "Caltex" for use on watches and allied goods. The respondent, the Commissioner of Central Excise, disputed the retrospective effect of the registration certificate, alleging wrongful use of another company's trademark, M/s. Ltd.
