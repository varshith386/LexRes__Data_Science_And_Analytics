The appeals arise from a High Court judgment dated 25-11-2009 concerning writ petitions challenging the acquisition of agricultural lands by the State of Uttar Pradesh for constructing a district jail in Shahjahanpur. The appellants, landowners in village Murchha, Shahjahanpur, contended that their fertile agricultural lands were acquired without proper adherence to procedural safeguards under the Land Acquisition Act, 1894 ("the Act").
