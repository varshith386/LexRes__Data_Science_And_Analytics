Parties: The appellant is Infosys Technologies Ltd. The first respondent is Jupiter Infosys Ltd., now Jupiter International Ltd.
Trademark Dispute: Infosys Technologies Ltd. had registered trademarks in classes 79 and 16.
Applications for Rectification: The first respondent filed applications under Sections 46 and 56 for removal of these trademarks on the grounds of non-use and incorrect registration.
IPAB Decision: The IPAB ordered the removal of the trademarks.
