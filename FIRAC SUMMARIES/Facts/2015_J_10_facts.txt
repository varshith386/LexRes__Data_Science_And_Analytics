The incident occurred on 18th November 1994, where the complainant and his sons were cutting pullas in their field.
Accused individuals, armed with weapons, confronted the complainant party over the cutting of pullas.
Altercation ensued, leading to physical assault by the accused on the complainant and his sons, resulting in serious injuries.
Sukhbir, one of the complainant's sons, suffered severe head injuries requiring urgent medical intervention.
