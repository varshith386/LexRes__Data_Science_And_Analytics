The incident took place involving an altercation at a residence where accused No. 3 and two juvenile offenders allegedly attacked the deceased.
The incident occurred in the presence of eyewitnesses who were inmates of the house.
Police received a cryptic information initially and reached the scene promptly.
FIR was registered at 3:00 AM after the complainant and witnesses were brought to the police station for proper recording due to poor visibility at the scene.
Medical evidence indicated the deceased suffered fatal stab wounds, with discrepancies between ocular and medical evidence regarding the number of injuries.
Accused was found with a knife used in the stabbing.
