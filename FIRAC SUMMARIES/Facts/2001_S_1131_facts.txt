Kumari S, a 5-year-old child, went missing on March 18, 1991, and her dead body was discovered the next day near Mohalla Basera in Village Kotputli.
Medical examination confirmed brutal sexual assault followed by murder.
The accused, Subhash, was arrested on April 3, 1991, after being implicated through circumstantial evidence including last seen together, abnormal conduct, and recovery of stained clothes.
