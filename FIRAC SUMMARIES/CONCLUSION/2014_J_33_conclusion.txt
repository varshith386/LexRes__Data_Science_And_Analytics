The Supreme Court set aside the High Court's judgment and remitted the matter to the District Collector, Meerut, to determine the Company's liabilities up to the date of asset vesting.
It directed the authorities to adjust any dues payable by the Company against the compensation amount and specified the process for payment or recovery accordingly.
The Court affirmed the entitlement of auction purchasers to interest on the refunded amount, citing equitable considerations and legal precedents.
