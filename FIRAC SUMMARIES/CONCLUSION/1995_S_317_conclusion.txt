The Court upheld the convictions of appellants Bonkya, Mandu, Ashok, Ranjar, and Kaka under IPC sections 302, 307, 149, and 324 based on evidence establishing their involvement in the assault resulting in Popat's death and injuries to PW9 and PW14. Appellant Mandu Baliba Dombe (A-10) was acquitted due to insufficient evidence linking him to the assault.
