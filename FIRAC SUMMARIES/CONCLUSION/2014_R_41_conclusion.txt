The court dismissed the appeals of Shashi Tripathi, Raju Devendra Choubey, and Binu Chandra Prakash, upholding their convictions under Section 302 read with Sections 34 and 120B of IPC.
Mahesh's appeal was allowed, and his conviction was set aside due to insufficient evidence linking him to the crime.
The court emphasized the importance of reliable eyewitness testimony and the necessity for corroboration in identifying accused persons, adhering to the principles of criminal law and evidence.
