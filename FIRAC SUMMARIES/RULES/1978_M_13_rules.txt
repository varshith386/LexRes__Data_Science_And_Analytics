Section 24(1) of the Bombay Merged Territories and Areas Jagirs Abolition Act, 1953: Provides for the determination of whether a jagir is proprietary or non-proprietary.
Section 37(2) of the Bombay Land Revenue Code: Authorizes the Collector to conduct an inquiry into claims regarding property rights, subject to a valid claim being put forward.
Section 10 of the Bombay Merged Territories and Areas Jagirs Abolition Act, 1953: Saves the rights of jagirdars to mines and mineral products granted or recognized under any contract, grant, law, or custom.
