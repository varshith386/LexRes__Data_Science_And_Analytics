Section 337 of the CrPC: Discusses the conditions under which a pardon may be granted to an accomplice and the authority empowered to grant it, ensuring it aligns with the gravity and trial jurisdiction of the offenses involved.

Sections 34, 114, 159 of the Indian Evidence Act: Concerns the relevance of entries in account books, the absence of entries as evidence, and the permissible use of documents to refresh the memory of a witness during testimony.
