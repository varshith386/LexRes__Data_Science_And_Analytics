Section 25 of the Patents Act, 1970 (as amended):

Pre-2005 Amendment: Allowed only pre-grant opposition.
Post-2005 Amendment: Introduced both pre-grant and post-grant opposition.
Section 116 of the Patents Act, 1970:

Allowed appeals against decisions of the Controller to the High Court prior to the 2005 amendment.
Post-2005 amendment (but before 02.04.2007), it was to be replaced with Section 117A providing appeals to the Appellate Board.
Section 117A (Post-2005 Amendment but effective from 02.04.2007):

Restricted appeals to the Appellate Board only for post-grant opposition orders.
Section 61 of the Patents Amendment Act, 2005:

Dealt with the transfer of pending proceedings to the Appellate Board.
