Definition of Partnership: As per Section 4 of the Indian Partnership Act, 1932, partnership involves an agreement to share profits of a business carried on by all or any of them acting for all.
Subletting under Rent Act: Section 131E of the Bombay Rent Act deals with subletting and its implications.
High Court's Revisional Power: Section 292 of the Bombay Rent Act grants the High Court the power to revise decisions to ensure they are according to law.
