Section 121A of the Karnataka Land Reforms Act, 1974: Grants the High Court the power to call for records of any order or proceeding recorded by the appellate authority to satisfy itself as to the legality of such order or regularity of such proceeding and may pass such orders as it deems fit.
Article 136 of the Constitution of India: Provides the Supreme Court the discretionary power to grant special leave to appeal against any judgment, decree, determination, sentence, or order in any cause or matter passed or made by any court or tribunal.
