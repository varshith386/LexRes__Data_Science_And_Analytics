Section 304-B IPC: Deals with dowry death and requires proof that the death of a woman was caused by burns, bodily injury, or under abnormal circumstances within seven years of marriage and that she was subjected to cruelty or harassment by her husband or his relatives in connection with any demand for dowry.
Section 2 of the Dowry Prohibition Act, 1961: Defines dowry as any property or valuable security given or agreed to be given directly or indirectly at or before or after the marriage in connection with the marriage of the parties.
Section 161 Cr.P.C: Relates to the recording of statements by police during an investigation.
