Issue 1: The court affirmed that the scheme from J.P. Unni Krishnan applies to minority educational institutions, specifying that admissions must prioritize merit while allowing for management discretion within defined quotas.
Issue 2: It clarified that the determination of minority status is subject to verification by state governments, especially where institutions receive aid.
Interim Orders: The court issued interim directives for admissions based on the existing scheme to ensure admissions proceed smoothly for the next academic session.
