Warning and Opportunity: The respondent received warnings from the Board regarding his unauthorized absence, fulfilling the requirement under Regulation No. 113.
High Court's Decision: The High Court's finding that no warning was issued based on the alleged non-receipt of a registered letter was rebutted by evidence showing the issuance and attempted delivery of warnings.
Jurisdiction of the Supreme Court: The Supreme Court reviewed whether the discharge was lawful under the rules and whether the High Court's decision to grant only partial relief was justified.
